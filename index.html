Classic free bodyweight training

Simple workouts. Real progress. No gym required.

SimpleFit combines bodyweight exercises, short circuit-training sessions, natural nutrition, and community support. This rebuild preserves the classic program and adds an offline workout companion you can install on your phone.

How it works

  1. Choose a level that fits your current ability.
  2. Do Day 1, Day 2, and Day 3 each week.
  3. Log your rounds or time.
  4. Move up if workouts are too easy; move down if they take too long.

A note on preservation

SimpleFit was once a popular community site and forum. This version keeps the content alive, points community discussion to Reddit, and recreates the missing workout-log experience as a private local-first app.

SimpleFit is free, community-preserved, and works offline. Consult your physician before starting any fitness program. If something hurts, stop.
